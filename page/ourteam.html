OUR TEAM

DR. NIRMAL CHORARIA
CEO & Chairman

Dr. Nirmal G. Choraria, Chairman and Chief Pediatrician, nirmal hospital pvt. ltd. is a eminent personality in gujarat. He is the founder president of the Perinatology forum surat which was established in 1996. Dr. Choraria has been the president of IMA, IAP and surat medical consultants association of surat.

In addition to his academics and professional achievements and contributions, he is a motivational speaker and has delivered many lectures in surat and south gujarat on different aspects of life.

MR. VINOD CHORARIA
Finance Consultant & Vice-Chairman

Mr. Vinod Choraria is Finance Consultant for HospiNet and Vice – Chairman of nirmal hospital private limited.

He has experience of working in the field of finance and hospital operations for more Than 30 years.

He has been a backbone to nirmal hospital private limited and his contribution has facilitated nirmal hospital group to explore and start new ventures and achieve great heights.

MR. JATIN JOSHI
Group COO

He is a strategic planner with proficiency in streamlining processes, defining continuous improvement processes, accelerating employees’ strengths and building powerful teams that can conquer any obstacles. He had received many leadership awards like FMCE leadership awards, SKOTCH award, ELETS award etc.

DR. PRADEEP PETHE
Medical Advisor

Dr. Pradeep Pethe was the first neurosurgeon of south gujarat, and for many years was the only neurosurgeon between ahmadabad and mumbai.

Experience of more than 8000 Cranial and Spinal surgeries. Stopped surgical practice in 2007 but continued consultations at private clinic.

Associated with nirmal hospital since 2001 when the hospital was converted from pediatric to general multispecialty hospital, first as neurosurgeon and then as medical advisor since 2007.

Initiated and led a voluntary organization ‘Aarambh’ to work with people with AIDS and their affected families from 2001 to 2012. Presently, honorary secretary of “Surat Raktadan Kendra and Research Center”.

Having 20 years of experience in Health Care Domains, Insurance, Mass Assurance and Finance.

He is a strategic planner with, defining continuous improvement processes, accelerating employees’ strengths and building powerful teams that can conquer any obstacles. He had received many leadership awards like FMCE leadership awards, SKOTCH award, ELETS award etc.
