PATHOLOGY

In its quest to provide perfect diagnosis, thus leading to quality patient care the hospital is ably supported by the pathology department. The department has a qualified team of pathologist, microbiologist, lab technicians and phlebotomist along with state of the art equipment.

  • Services provided 24*7 functioning with no emergency charges during nights and Sundays / public holidays.
  • Home collection facilities available.
  • Department is equipped with following instruments.
  • AIA 360-Hormone analyzer
  • Hematology analyzer
  • COBAS – b121 – ABG analyzer
  • EM – 360 fully auto biochem analyzer
  • ERBA – ELAN 30s, ELISA reader
  • BIORAD – D- 10
  • BACT/ALERT
  • VITEK
  • Laboratory is NABH accredited
  • QC practice followed daily and monitoring of the same done.
  • Reports are dispatched within the defined TAT
  • Safety precaution laid down by NABH is followed.
  • Ongoing teaching and upgrading of the department is carried out.
